About us

We specialize in the fabrication, scenics, mould, and casting of display products and set building for film and television. We create bespoke pieces and ambiance to help bring your conceptual ideas to life. Mostly based in Johannesburg and Capetown, but can work anywhere in the country or abroad.

We have produced exclusive work for the biggest film productions in the
country and our services include:
Fabricating
Mould and casting
Scenics
Wallpaper fitting
painting (industrial and residential)
